Mind Trekkers is a flagship program of Michigan Technological University's Center for Pre-College outreach. Mind Trekkers utilizes Michigan Tech undergraduate and graduate students to travel all across the country to deliver high-energy, hands-on, STEM demonstrations to K-12 students and inspire them to pursure post-secondary education. Have you been inside a bubble? Can you skewer a balloon without popping it? Have you ever played a piano made of bananas? Let Mind Trekkers be your guide as you explore the excitement of STEM.
